About SCENE TEXT

Empowering AI to read the world around us.

What is Scene Text Recognition?

Scene Text Recognition (STR) detects and recognizes text from real-world environments such as street signs and product labels, helping AI understand the visual world.

Why is Scene Text Recognition Important?

  • Augmented Reality: Enhances user experience through text overlays.
  • Autonomous Vehicles: Reads road signs and aids in navigation.
  • Accessibility: Assists visually impaired users by reading real-world text aloud.
  • Content Search: Enables text-based searches in image databases.

Challenges in Scene Text Recognition

  • Diverse fonts, colors, and styles.
  • Cluttered or noisy backgrounds.
  • Perspective distortions.
  • Real-time performance constraints.

How Does STR Work?

  1. Text Detection
  2. Text Localization
  3. Text Recognition (via deep learning models)
  4. Post-processing & optimization

Real-World Applications

  • Extracting text from images/videos
  • Business card scanners & translation apps
  • Smart city implementations
